VISION

JRU will be a market leader in the use of technology for innovation in teaching and learning to produce graduates of social importance.

MISSION

The University aims to develop its students to become useful and responsible citizens through the effective transfer of (relevant knowledge and desirable values.

INSTITUTIONAL OUTCOME

A JRU graduate has the competencies and values in the disciplinal area completed such that he/she is a useful and responsible citizen of the country.

SUBJECT DESCRIPTION

Work Immersion is one of the course requirements for graduation This course is equivalent to the Work Immersion of STEM strand. It will provide STEM students the opportunity to integrate their learning from Allied Sciences subjects. It focuses on the exhibitions of authentic outcomes and performances as evidence of their learning. Students are required to accomplish an 80-hour period that will enable them to fulfill the objectives of the subject. These prepare them to meet the needs and challenges of employment or higher education after graduation.

Specifically, the students are able to:

1. Enhance their technical knowledge and skills.

2. Enrich their skills in communications and human relations.

3. Develop good work habits, attitudes, appreciation, and respect for work.
